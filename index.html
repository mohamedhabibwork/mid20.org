ABOUT US

In the mid-twenties, where uncertainty meets opportunities.

Welcome to Mid20, an initiative passionately dedicated to navigating the challenges faced by the vibrant youth of Egypt in their mid-twenties. In this pivotal phase of life, young adults often find themselves at a crossroads, grappling with questions about their life goals, career paths, and personal development. At Mid20, we recognize this unique "crisis of the mid-twenties" and are committed to providing tailored support to empower individuals to pursue their dreams effectively.

Challenge Overview

Main Challenge

In the dynamic landscape of Egypt, the mid-twenties present a defining period for young individuals, marked by a complex array of challenges that can shape their personal and professional trajectories. Aptly termed "The crisis of the mid-twenties," this phase often involves the struggle to articulate life goals, aspirations, and a clear path forward. MiD-20 was born out of the recognition that the absence of proper guidance and support during this crucial time can lead to a generation feeling directionless and unfulfilled.

Vision & Mission

Vision

Empowering Mid-Twenties for a Purposeful Tomorrow
Mid20 envisions a future where the mid-twenties phase becomes a transformative journey, paving the way for confident, resilient, and purpose-driven individuals who positively shape their communities.

Mission

Guiding, Mentoring, and Empowering the Mid-Twenties Generation
Mid20 provides tailored guidance, mentorship, and skill development to navigate the mid-twenties crisis. Our mission is to foster a supportive community that empowers young adults, helping them discover their potential and embark on a journey of personal and professional growth.

Slogan

Paving the Way
Our slogan, "Paving the Way for Your Journey," represents Mid20's commitment to clearing the path for young adults, ensuring they confidently stride into a future of possibilities and fulfilment.
